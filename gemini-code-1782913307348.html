Maker Persona

I am a student maker who learns by building things that create meaningful experiences. I believe that things built for people to experience should be just as fun and exciting as it was for the designer. From systems and interfaces to small experiments, I enjoy learning about how they work and try to redesign them in an easier way. I started designing through webpage layouts and carried that forward into other niches like physical hardware, mockups, and packaging design.

Kinematic Cinema Rigs MAR 2026

RIG Series: Mobile Filmmaking Tools

An integration ecosystem aiming to unlock cinema production parameters out of standard consumer smartphones. Designed to explore physical layout limits and external manufacturing design alignment parameters.

System Architecture Elements:

Part-1: The Phone Cage: An elastic, ultra-strong lightweight phone cage designed to maximize security while nullifying size variance constraints.

Part-2: The Handheld H-RIG: A lightweight structural PVC frame built to ensure clean dual-handed stabilization adjustments across track sequences.

Part-3: The Smart Tabletop T-RIG: A highly sophisticated stepper-motor rig tracking system utilizing microcontrollers, an integrated ESP32 Wi-Fi relay, and joystick adjustments—optionally guided autonomously via Google's Machine Learning Kit framework.
